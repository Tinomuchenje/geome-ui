Genomic Observatories MetaDatabase (GEOME)

The Genomic Observatories Meta-Database (GEOME) is a web-based database which captures metadata on biological samples and associated genetic sequences. GEOME captures the who, what, where, and when associated with all samples and enables loading and linking sequence data, photos, and tissues. When working with metadata for FASTQ files from massively parallel sequencing runs, GEOME produces a submission package to ease the upload process into NCBIâ€™s Sequence Read Archive (SRA) and creates a permanent link between the metadata and the data.

Why Use GEOME?

  • Ensure the metadata from your biological samples is findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable (FAIR)
  • Improve the quality of your data
  • Expedite submission to the Sequence Read Archive (SRA) and maintain links between the sample and sequence data
  • Comply with global data standards for geographic, biodiversity, and sequence metadata
  • Integrate with the Biocode Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) plugin to the Geneious software
