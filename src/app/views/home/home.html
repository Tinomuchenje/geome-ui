Genomic Observatories MetaDatabase (GeOMe)

The Genomic Observatories Meta-Database (GeOMe) is a web-based database which captures metadata on biological samples. GeOMe satisfies the requirements of the genomic observatories model, including capturing the who, what, where, and when associated with all samples. In addition to validating sample-based metadata, GeOMe enables loading FASTA files, photos, and 96-well plate-based tissue matching.
